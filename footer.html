James Doy is a User Experience Designer. He creates great looking and easy to use mobile and desktop experiences and interfaces.

His former careers include a year long stint as an apprentice fork-lift truck mechanic, a barman in the third most popular bar in Lowestoft and a brief, but eventful week as a New York City courier. Mostly though, he's been a designer since 2008.

Despite loving things that work properly, he also owns a small British car made in the 90s.

James has worked both client and agency side with brands such as: Siemens, Argos, Wickes, Office Depot, Tails.com, Mitsubishi Motors, HSBC, Sky, HERE Technologies and Your Parking Space.
